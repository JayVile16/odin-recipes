BBQ Chicken

Description

A recipe that I took from my mother. It uses my personal favorite store brand of BBQ sauce, Sweet Baby Ray's. The chicken by itself is heavily seasoned. The BBQ sauce adds a wonderful juiciness and complements the seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 6-12 Chicken Drumsticks
  • 3-6 Chicken Thighs
  • 2 Tbsps of Honey
  • 1-2 bottles of Honey BBQ (Personally recommend Sweet Baby Ray's
  • Aluminum Pan

Directions

  1. Clean chicken thoroughly with distilled vinegar and lemon juice, pat dry when done.
  2. Evenly distribute the Thighs and Drumsticks on the pan
  3. Preheat oven to 425 Degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Season chicken with desired spices (recommend paprika)
  5. Place chicken in oven for an hour covered.
  6. Uncover chicken, take half a cup of the water from the pan.
  7. Mix BBQ, Honey, and chicken stock in a bowl
  8. Place BBQ mix on uncovered chicken, put chicken back in the oven for half an hour
