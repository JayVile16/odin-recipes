Mac and Cheese

Description

A modern classic, Mac and Cheese. Seasoned with a variety of spices (IMPORTANT Cajun/Creole Seasoning is must). Make sure the blocks of cheese are shredded. I do not recommend pre shredded cheese but you can use it if you are pressed for time.

Ingredients

  • A box of desired pasta
  • Heavy Cream/Half & Half or any other type of milk or milk alternative
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 LB of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 LB of Pepper Jack Cheese
  • 1 LB of Mozzerela Cheese
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Cajun Style Seasoning
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Shred or blocks of cheese
  3. Heat up a large sauce pan. Once heated enough start gradually pouring in creamer/milk. Stir as you pour
  4. Follow directions on back of pasta box to cook it, save a little of pasta water for cheese sauce
  5. Season heated mix with desired spices. Pour in half of shredded cheese amount. Pour in pasta water. Stir mixture thoroughly.
  6. Once mix is desired taste, combine pasta with cheese mix. Mix thoroughly.
  7. Pour pasta into baking pan, top with the the rest of shredded cheese
  8. Place pan in oven and cook for 30-45 mins.
  9. Enjoy!
